## Reiwa 7 Exam Overview

The Reiwa 7 (2025) IPA public exam consists of 100 questions. The exam format is CBT, administered year-round, with no distinction between spring and fall sessions. The passing criteria require a total score of 60% or higher, plus a minimum of 30% in each category. This article presents the results of analyzing the 100-question IPA sample by field and keyword.

## Question Distribution by Field (Reiwa 7)

| Field | Number of Questions | Percentage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Strategy | 35 | 35% |
| Management | 20 | 20% |
| Technology | 45 | 45% |

Since Reiwa 3, the 35:20:45 ratio has been completely fixed. Since Technology accounts for the largest volume, you should allocate the most study resources there.

## Number of Questions by Keyword (Reiwa 7)

Security had 9 questions, remaining the most frequent area as in previous years. Many questions covered types of cyber attacks, encryption, and authentication. AI had 7 questions, the highest in the past five years, with an increase in generative AI-related questions. Project management had 6 questions, covering PMBOK, WBS, and stakeholders. Cloud had 2 questions on identifying IaaS/PaaS/SaaS, and authentication had 2 questions on MFA and single sign-on. Encryption, IoT, Agile, RPA, and risk management each had 1 question. Meanwhile, personal information, BCP, and SDGs each had 0 questions, showing decreased attention compared to the previous year.

## Characteristics of Reiwa 7

### Significant Decrease in Diagram Questions

Over the past five years, the number of questions including diagrams was 2 in 2021, 6 in 2022, 2 in 2023, and 3 in 2024, but dropped to 0 in 2025. Since more questions are now text-only, long-form reading comprehension has become relatively more important.

### Sharp Increase in AI-Related Weight

AI, which had 3 to 5 questions per year from Reiwa 3 to 6, increased to 7 questions in Reiwa 7. Risks of generative AI, particularly hallucinations, copyright issues, and confidential information leaks, were frequently tested. For details, see [AI and Machine Learning Basics](/blog/ai-ml-basics).

### Security Remains a Heavyweight

Security is still the largest keyword area. For combination questions on attack names and countermeasures, check the [Summary of Cyber Attack Types](/blog/cyber-attacks) for study strategies.

## Top 5 Frequent Topics in Reiwa 7

1. **Security (9 questions)** — Classification of cyber attacks, encryption methods, authentication methods
2. **AI (7 questions)** — Classification of machine learning, risks of generative AI
3. **Project Management (6 questions)** — PMBOK, WBS, Gantt charts
4. **Cloud, IoT, Data Utilization (around 5 questions)** — Questions in the context of DX
5. **Accounting and Business Strategy (around 10 questions)** — Break-even point, frameworks

## Study Priorities

First, aim to reliably score points on the 16 questions from Security and AI in the Technology field. Next, focus on Strategy frameworks and accounting ([Break-Even Point](/blog/break-even), [Differences Between SWOT, 3C, and PEST Analysis](/blog/swot-3c-pest)). For the Management field, study centered on [PMBOK, WBS, and Gantt Charts](/blog/pmbok-wbs).
