## Prerequisites for the Reiwa 6 Exam

The IPA public exam for Reiwa 6 (2024) consists of 100 questions. The exam format is the year-round CBT method, with no distinction between spring and fall sessions. The passing criteria are set at a total score of 60% or higher and at least 30% in each field. This analysis breaks down the 100-question sample by field and keyword.

## Distribution of Questions by Field (Reiwa 6)

| Field | Number of Questions | Percentage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Strategy | 35 questions | 35% |
| Management | 20 questions | 20% |
| Technology | 45 questions | 45% |

Since Reiwa 3, the 35:20:45 ratio has been completely fixed. The Reiwa 7 public exam follows the same structure. For details, refer to the [Reiwa 7 analysis article](/blog/reiwa7-analysis).

## Number of Questions by Keyword for Reiwa 6

By keyword, security had the most questions at 8, making it the most frequent field as usual. Project management had 6 questions, covering PMBOK and WBS. AI had 5 questions, showing an increasing trend toward 7 questions in Reiwa 7. RPA had 3 questions, authentication (MFA and SSO) also had 3, and IoT had 2, which was low. Encryption, cloud, personal information, and agile each had 1 question, while BCP was not tested.

## Characteristics of Reiwa 6

### More Project Management Questions

Out of the 20 total Management questions, 6 were related to project management. Specifically, topics included WBS, stakeholder management, and risk management.

### Many RPA Questions

Reiwa 6 had a relatively high number of RPA questions at 3, with half of the 6 questions from the past five years concentrated in this year. This likely reflects the trend toward business automation. For more details, check [RPA and Business Automation](/blog/rpa-automation).

### Diagram Questions: 3 Questions

There were 3 diagram questions, which is relatively high compared to 0 in Reiwa 7. This is a slight increase from 2 questions in Reiwa 5.

### Increase in AI-Related Questions

AI-related questions have increased from 3-4 per year in Reiwa 3-5 to 5 in Reiwa 6, and 7 in Reiwa 7. This is thought to reflect the impact of the spread of generative AI.

## Study Priority for Reiwa 6

As a study priority, first secure the 14 questions from security and project management. Next, focus on the 8 questions from AI and RPA. Then, studying the Strategy field's business and accounting topics is efficient.
