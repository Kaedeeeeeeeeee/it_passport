## Basic Exam Registration Information

Registration for the IT Passport exam is done through the official website of the Information-technology Promotion Agency (IPA). The exam fee is **7,500 yen (tax included)** as of the 2024 revision. Payment methods include credit card, convenience store payment, Pay-easy, and vouchers (such as student discounts). The exam is offered year-round via CBT, allowing you to take it at a time that suits you.

## Registration Procedure (5 Steps)

### Step 1: Create a User ID

First, access the "User Menu" on the IPA official website and register your email address, name, address, and other details. After registration, click the URL in the confirmation email to complete your registration.

### Step 2: Obtain a Prometric ID

The IT Passport CBT exam is operated by Prometric. A linked ID is automatically issued when you complete your user registration. Once registered, you can reuse the same ID for subsequent exams.

### Step 3: Choose Your Exam Date and Test Center

Select your exam date and test center from Prometric's authorized locations nationwide. In urban areas, weekend slots tend to fill up quickly, so aiming for weekday or early-morning slots makes it easier to find availability. Options expand if you look across different months.

### Step 4: Pay the Exam Fee

| Payment Method | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| Credit Card | Instant confirmation, easiest option |
| Convenience Store Payment | A payment slip is issued |
| Pay-easy | Online banking or ATM |
| Voucher | Prepaid via student discount or corporate contract |

Your reservation is confirmed once payment is complete.

### Step 5: Get Your Admission Ticket (Confirmation Slip)

The reservation confirmation email contains a link to your admission ticket. Printing is not required; showing the email screen is acceptable. Be sure to bring it along with your identification documents on the exam day.

## Important Notes for Registration

### Accuracy of Name and Date of Birth

The name and date of birth on your admission ticket must exactly match those on your identification documents. Be careful, as discrepancies—such as using a former surname or differences in romanized spelling—may prevent you from taking the exam.

### Cancellation and Change Rules

You can change or cancel your reservation free of charge up to 3 days before the exam date. Within 3 days, fees may apply or changes may become impossible. Since rules are subject to change, always check the latest information.

### Using Student Discounts

You may be able to obtain a discounted exam fee voucher through your school or educational institution. For details, check with your academic advisor or refer to [IT Passport Student Discounts and Fee Reduction Programs](/blog/exam-discount).

## After Registration – Exam Day

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q. Can I take the exam multiple times?

There is no limit on the number of attempts, but the exam fee applies each time.

### Q. Can I cancel on the day?

As a rule, same-day cancellations are not possible. The exam fee will not be refunded.

### Q. What if I lose my admission ticket?

You can re-display or reprint it from the user menu.

### Q. What time should I arrive at the test center?

Arriving 30 minutes before the exam start time is recommended.
