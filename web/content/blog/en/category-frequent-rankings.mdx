## Frequently Tested Topics in the Strategy Field (35 questions × 5 years = 175 questions)

### Business Strategy

In business strategy, SWOT, 3C, and PEST analyses are frequently tested. Understand the differences between these frameworks. Also, the Five Forces analysis and PPM are commonly asked. Don't overlook questions about business models.

### Marketing

In the marketing field, the 4P and 4C marketing mixes are important. Be sure to grasp the concepts of segmentation, targeting, and positioning.

### Accounting & Finance

In accounting and finance, break-even point calculation problems are frequently tested. Profitability indicators like ROE and ROA are also commonly asked.

### Legal & Compliance

In the legal field, the basics of copyright law are frequently tested. The Act on the Protection of Personal Information is also an important topic. Understand the differences between the four types of industrial property rights.

### DX & New Concepts

Questions about DX (Digital Transformation) are increasing. Also, the concepts of SDGs and ESG management are within the exam scope.

## Frequently Tested Topics in the Management Field (20 questions × 5 years = 100 questions)

### Project Management

In project management, PMBOK, WBS, and PERT are the core topics. Stakeholder management and risk management are also important themes.

### System Development

In system development, the differences between waterfall and agile are frequently tested. Grasp the overall flow of the system development process. Knowledge of testing techniques is also necessary.

### Service Management

In service management, ITIL concepts are asked. Understanding SLA and calculating availability rates also require preparation.

### Audit & Control

The basics of system audit and internal control are frequently tested. Also, be sure to cover the outline of the J-SOX Act.

## Frequently Tested Topics in the Technology Field (45 questions × 5 years = 225 questions)

### Security (Most Frequent)

Security is the most frequently tested theme across all fields. Memorize the types of cyber attacks. The difference between encryption and authentication/authorization is also important. Understand the roles of firewalls, WAF, and IDS/IPS.

### Networking

In the networking field, the seven layers of the OSI reference model are frequently tested. Also, study the basics of the TCP/IP protocol thoroughly.

### Databases

For databases, the basics of relational databases and SQL are tested. Knowledge of normalization is also essential.

### AI & New Technologies

The basics of AI and machine learning are frequently tested. IoT concepts are also commonly asked. Be sure to understand the mechanism of blockchain.

### Computer Fundamentals

In computer fundamentals, binary/hexadecimal numbers and logical operations are tested. Also, memorize the differences between CPU, memory, and storage.

## How to Set Study Priorities

### 1st Priority (Essential)

The first priority is security in general. With 8 to 9 questions per year, it is essential. AI and IoT are also frequently tested with 5 to 10 questions per year, so prioritize studying them.

### 2nd Priority (High Score Source)

The second priority includes networking (OSI, TCP/IP). Databases (SQL, normalization) are also a high score source. Don't forget to prepare for project management.

### 3rd Priority (Differentiator)

The third priority includes business strategy and accounting from the Strategy field. Audit and SLA from the Management field are also topics that can set you apart.
